About Us

At Amir Steel, we are more than just a name in the steel manufacturing and trading industry. We are a second-generation family business with over 12 years of experience in providing high-quality SS Circles and SS Coils to various industries. Based in Vasai Golani, we specialize in creating durable steel products that contribute to a wide range of applications, including the manufacturing of steel utensils and other essential steel components.

Our Story

Our journey began with a vision to provide high-quality stainless steel products that meet the ever-evolving needs of the market. Over the years, our commitment to excellence has helped us build lasting relationships with clients across diverse sectors. We pride ourselves on being a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable and cost-effective solutions in the steel industry.

Whether you need SS coils for fabrication or SS circles for specialized applications, Amir Steel has been the go-to supplier for industry leaders who demand the best. As we continue to grow and evolve, we remain dedicated to our legacy of delivering unmatched quality and service to our customers.
